Turkey

A turkey, pre-cooking

Ingredients

  • One big turkey
  • Salt + pepper
  • Butter
  • Herbs + Spices

Steps

  1. Kill the turkey
  2. de-feather and clean the turkey
  3. apologize to the turkey
  4. remove the turkey's spine and break its breast bone
  5. place the turkey flat on a baking sheet w a wire rack
  6. salt the turkey all over
  7. leave the turkey alone for awhile
  8. butter that baby up
  9. add pepper and herbs and spices
  10. preheat dat oven
  11. cook until its done-probably 20 degrees cooler than whatever your mom tells you
  12. carve + eat!
