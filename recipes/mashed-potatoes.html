Mashed Potatoes

Potatoes, pre-mashing

Ingredients

  • Some potatoes
  • Salt + pepper
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Garlic
  • Herbs if you want

Steps

  1. Kidnap potatoes from the earth
  2. scrub all that dirt off
  3. peel their skin off, especially any spots or sprouts
  4. chop em up into equal size pieces
  5. place pieces in boiling salty water
  6. boil until your knife slips right through em
  7. strain in a collander
  8. put back in the pot
  9. cook a lil minced garlic in butter
  10. pour garlic butter into pot w potatoes
  11. mash up w a masher
  12. add milk or cream as you like for creamy texture
  13. salt + pepper till tasty
  14. add some freshed chopped herbs if you want - dill or parsley are nice
  15. eat in a bowl or on a plate w a fork or a spoon w/e u like
