Stuffing

Stuffing, pre-stuffing

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Salt + pepper
  • Chicken Stock
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Carrots + celery
  • And onion too
  • Sausage
  • Herbs if you want

Steps

  1. let your bread get all stale, or bake it after step 2 if you're in a hurry
  2. chop bread into cubes
  3. cook sausage in a dutch oven, then set aside leaving the pork fat in
  4. chop up carrots and celery into lil bits ooh and onion too.
  5. cook them babies down till they're translucent. add some garlic.
  6. pop your bread cubes in there
  7. heat your chicken stock up in a pot starting like 15 mins ago
  8. ladle stock one scoop at a time into the bread risotto style
  9. mix that baby up keeping an eye on the consistency
  10. when its like halfway where you want it add a couple pats of buttter and the sausage back in
  11. keep on scooping broth and mixing that pudding
  12. when its like 90% done add some salt and pepper to taste
  13. if ur a lil freak let the moisture on the bottom boil off and let it get a lil crust
  14. oh add some herbs like sage sage is important. ideally w the sausage so everything is sagey
  15. eat in a bowl or on a plate w a fork or a spoon w/e u like
